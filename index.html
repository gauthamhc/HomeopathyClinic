Dr V Phanindranath

2002: batch Homoeopathic Graduate from Government Homoeopathic Medical College.
2002 - 2004 : Underwent POST GRADUATE REGULAR TRAINING PROGRAMME at M L Dhawale Memorial Rural Homoeopathic Hospital, Palghar, Thane District, Maharashtra.
2005- till date : Private Homoeopathic Practice. ... 2007-2009: Served as a Rural Community Medical officer at MLDMHI, Bengaluru Branch. 2010 - Worked with CHILDREN with SPECIAL NEEDS in association with TAMAHAR- Centre for children with special needs. 2014 onwards have been associated with Sri Sathya Sai Seva Samithi at Dandeli and Karwar. We conduct monthly FREE RURAL MEDICAL CAMPS at Domagera Village - 1st Sunday & on 3rd Sunday at Hireshetta Village at Karwar. In these camps we get to see an average of 150 patients of various clinical diagnosis. We have been treating & managing patients from all age groups for various complaints. We believe in continuously supporting patients and hold HOMOEOPATHIC MEDICINES as FAMILY MEDICINE....
